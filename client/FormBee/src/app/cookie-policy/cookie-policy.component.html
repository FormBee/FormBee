Cookie Policy

Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies when you use our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, like your preferred language and other settings.

Cookies We Use

We only use cookies related to our GitHub OAuth integration, and Stripe. Here are the details of each github related oauth cookies:

  • _octo - Identifies GitHub OAuth sessions.
  • color_mode - Stores your theme preference.
  • dotcom_user - Used for GitHub OAuth.
  • logged_in - Indicates if you are logged into GitHub.
  • preferred_color_mode - Stores preferred color mode.
  • tz - Stores time zone information.

Stripe cookies are used for fraud prevention and security purposes.

Managing Cookies

You can set your browser to not accept cookies.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
